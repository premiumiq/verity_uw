# Model Card

> **Tooltip:** Per-entity documentation of purpose, design, limitations, conditions of use, validation evidence (SR 11-7 style).

## Definition

A row in `model_card` providing the formal SR 11-7-style documentation for one entity: stated purpose, design rationale, known limitations, conditions of use, and validation evidence summary. Required at the `challenger → champion` gate for high-materiality entities.
